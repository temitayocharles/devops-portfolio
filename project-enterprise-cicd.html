The Challenge: Building Enterprise CI/CD

Enterprises struggle with manual deployments, inconsistent builds, and deployment bottlenecks

The Problem

Enterprise organizations struggle with manual deployment processes that are error-prone, time-consuming, and unreliable. Inconsistent build environments between dev and production cause "works on my machine" problems. Low deployment frequency limits feature velocity.

Pain Points

  • Manual Deployments: Scripts and manual procedures cause human errors and inconsistency
  • Inconsistent Environments: Dev/staging/prod diverge, causing deployment failures
  • Low Velocity: Deployment frequency is 1-2 times per week, limiting feature releases
  • No Rollback: Failed deployments are difficult to recover from, extending downtime
  • Compliance Gaps: Manual processes lack audit trails and approval workflows

Business Impact: Slow feature delivery, deployment failures, long incident recovery, security/compliance risks
