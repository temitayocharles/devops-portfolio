Twelve federal agencies were operating with completely different testing frameworks, each with their own tools, processes, and quality standards. This fragmentation created massive inefficiencies, inconsistent quality, and significant security risks across critical government systems.

The Problem: Testing framework fragmentation, inconsistent quality standards, redundant development efforts, and compliance gaps across federal agencies.

Critical Issues Identified:

  • Inconsistent quality standards and processes

The Promise:

  • 85% improvement in testing efficiency
  • $15M annual cost reduction
