The SRE Challenge: Manual Triage & Delayed Response

SRE teams spend 70% of incident response time on manual triage - searching logs, correlating metrics, researching runbooks, and identifying root causes. This intelligence platform automates initial triage using advanced LLM agents with vector search, helping reduce MTTR by up to 60% on recurring incident patterns and supporting proactive discovery of infrastructure vulnerabilities before they impact production.
