The Patient Adherence Challenge

A hospital network with 10,000+ patients faced critical medication adherence issues. 50% of patients were missing doses, leading to poor health outcomes, hospital readmissions, and $2.8M annual costs. Complex medication regimens, forgetfulness, and lack of personalized support were the main culprits.

The Problem: 50% medication non-adherence, poor patient outcomes, hospital readmissions, and $2.8M annual healthcare costs from missed medication management.

Critical Issues Identified:

  • 50% of patients missing critical medication doses
  • $2.8M annual costs from poor adherence outcomes
  • Complex medication regimens causing confusion
  • Lack of personalized patient support systems
  • Hospital readmissions due to medication non-adherence

The AI-Powered Solution

I designed and implemented DailyDose, an AI-powered medication reminder and adherence platform. The system achieved 95% adherence rates, reduced hospital readmissions by 40%, and saved $125K monthly through intelligent reminders, personalized support, and real-time monitoring.

The Promise:

  • 95% medication adherence through AI-powered reminders
  • $125K monthly savings from reduced readmissions
  • 24/7 personalized patient support and monitoring
  • Real-time medication tracking and compliance reporting
  • HIPAA-compliant patient data protection
